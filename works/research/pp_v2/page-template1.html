With us, your dissertation is written in following phases:

Preliminary phase:

  • Firstly, you are assigned with individual writer
  • Our writer receives all your needs and requirements for dissertation
  • Upon your necessity, we finish the first copy of your dissertation within one week
  • From first copy we further improve the dissertation by considering suggestions and expectations from you and your supervisor

Midterm phase:

  • Based on first copy, the dissertation of your research is organized
  • Dissertation writing begins on your university format
  • Entire dissertation is arranged chapter-by-chapter
  • Each chapter is completed after your approval to assure transparency in our work
  • Additional matters (indexing, graphical abstract, bibliography)

Endmost phase:

  • The completed dissertation is reviewed by our senior professionals
  • All suggestions gained during review process are addressed
