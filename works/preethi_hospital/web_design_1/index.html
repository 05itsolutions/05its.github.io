PIMS Multispeciality

Preethi Multispecialty hospitals Pvt Ltd. establised in Uthangudi, Madurai has a history of 20 years in medical services. Along with its basic specialities of orthopaedics and Gynecology in which it is the most reputed and advanced health care centre in southern Tamilnadu, it is now able to manage problems related to Oncology, Intensive Care, Renal transplant, Cardiac care and many more.

Our Quality Policy

To cater the health care system of international standards for all the needy patients in a comprehensive and genuine manner. Hospital will update the quality of service with innovative cutting edge technology and modern equipments. Also the new skills are inculcated among surgeons as well as hospital staffs.

Our Motto is "Quality based genuine medical care for all."
