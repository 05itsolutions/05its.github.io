Urology and Andrology

  • Urology
  • Andrology

Urology is a medical and surgical specialty that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of urinary tract of adults and children and reproductive system of males. Our hospital urologist is trained to treat patients with related disorders. The organs covered for treatment under this department include the kidneys, adrenal glands, ureters, urinary bladder, urethra and the male reproductive organs.

Our Urology department makes Preethi Hospital one of the best Urology centers of the city. It provides clinical and surgical assistance for all genitourinary disorders and deals with problems related to kidney, ureter & urinary bladder. Common diseases like prostate enlargement, urinary stones, strictures are treated by us in this department.

With the help of our state-of-the-art equipments and facilities, the urology unit offers minimally invasive, scarless options for most of the conditions. We are also the best providers of post operative care and management. We host the latest lithotripsy machine to treat patients with renal stones.

Various diseases and disorders treated in our Urology department

  • Urinary stones
  • Benign prostate Hypertrothy
  • Urinary Incontinence (A common problem in elderly people)
  • Prostate Cancer
  • Prostatitis
  • Female urology
  • Pediatric urology
